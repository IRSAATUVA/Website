IRSA PEOPLE.
IRSA is a nonprofit organization run by Iranian Students at the University of Virginia established in Fall 2016. Rotunda Lawn.
The Rotunda is a building located on The Lawn in the original grounds of the University of Virginia. It was designed by Thomas Jefferson to represent the "authority of nature and power of reason" and was inspired by the Pantheon in Rome. Shenandoah National Park.
Shenandoah National Park is a national park which encompasses part of the Blue Ridge Mountains in the U.S. state of Virginia. The park is long and narrow, with the broad Shenandoah River and Valley on the west side, and the rolling hills of the Virginia Piedmont on the east.

Immigration Teach-In UVA professor Panel
Please join a panel of professors for greater insight and discussion on the new immigration policy and its implications for us. The event will include a period for questions/answers, as well as individual talks by the attending professors. Mr. Mayor also will be joining us for this event. Professor Jahan Ramazani is also one of the very top professors at UVa. If you have any concerns/questions that you want to be answered please share your thoughts with us so we can include it in our discussion.
Date and time: Wednesday, Feb 1, 4:30-6:00 pm Location: Rotunda Multipurpose Room (in the southwest wing of the Rotunda)
